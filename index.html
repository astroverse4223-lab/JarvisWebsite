FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What operating systems are supported?

Currently, JARVIS Omega is available for Windows 10 and Windows 11. Mac and Linux support is planned for future releases.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! JARVIS works completely offline with the Basic plan. The Pro plan with AI can work offline with Ollama or online with OpenAI.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, absolutely. All voice processing happens locally on your machine. No data is sent to external servers unless you choose to use OpenAI.

Can I customize the commands?

Yes! You can add your own custom voice commands and Q&A responses through simple YAML configuration files.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10/11, 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended), microphone access, and ~300MB disk space.

Is there a refund policy?

NO REFUNDS. We offer a 3-day free trial so you can test all features risk-free before committing. After the trial, all sales are final.
