What is JARVIS Omega? ▼

JARVIS Omega is an advanced AI-powered voice assistant for Windows that allows you to control your PC using natural voice commands. Inspired by Iron Man's JARVIS, it features 8 stunning themes, 12+ powerful features including system control, app launching, web searches, and much more.

Unlike cloud-based assistants, JARVIS Omega processes most commands locally on your device, ensuring privacy and faster response times.

Why isn't JARVIS responding to my voice commands? ▼

Troubleshooting Steps:

  1. Check Microphone: Ensure your microphone is connected and set as default in Windows sound settings
  2. Permission Settings: Grant microphone permissions in Windows Privacy Settings
  3. Wake Word: Make sure you're saying the wake word "Jarvis" before commands
  4. Background Noise: Reduce ambient noise or move to a quieter location
  5. Recalibrate: Go to Settings > Voice > Recalibrate Voice Recognition
  6. Update: Ensure you have the latest version installed

If issues persist, contact our support team.

How do I customize voice commands? ▼

Custom Commands Setup:

  1. Open JARVIS Omega settings
  2. Navigate to Commands > Custom Commands
  3. Click "Add New Command"
  4. Enter your trigger phrase (e.g., "open my project")
  5. Choose action type: Launch App, Run Script, Execute Command, etc.
  6. Configure action parameters
  7. Save and test your command

Pro Tip: Use variables like {date}, {time}, {clipboard} in your commands for dynamic content!
